English

Chigusa: I can get winter bonus next month.
Yoshi: Same here! I'm looking forward to the next month. By the way, are you still planning the trip to Shikoku?
Chigusa: Yes, that's right! The average of the winter bonus last year was 675,000 yen, and it will increase this year, so I have enough money.
Yoshi: 675,000 yen!! I envy you. Our average was 65,000 yen. It will decrease this year, too, so I'm really sad.
