Kanji

よし: 僕があげた薔薇の木はどこ？
夏子: こっち。
よし: ひどい！！死にそう！これは僕らの愛の象徴だよ。
僕らの愛も死にそう？
夏子: 冬だから花も愛も枯れるのよ。
よし: じゃ、春には復活するんだね。
夏子: ええ、多分他の人とね。

English

Yoshi: Where's the rose bush I gave you?
Natsuko: Here.
Yoshi: That's terrible!! It looks like it's going to die! This is a symbol of our love. Is our love going to die, too?
Natsuko: In winter, flowers and love wither.
Yoshi: But, in the spring they come back.
Natsuko: Yeah, but maybe with someone else.
